Feathly Community

Get updates, ask public questions, share feedback, and help shape Smart Planner and future Feathly products.

Join DiscordPrivate Support

Discord is a public community space for announcements, product discussion, feedback, and general help. Billing, refunds, purchase records, privacy requests, and sensitive technical evidence must use the private Feathly support form.

What to share on Discord

  • Smart Planner feedback and general usage questions
  • Ideas for loop patterns, reminders, focus sessions, and future features
  • Public bug descriptions that do not include personal data
  • Product updates, release discussion, and community tips
  • Requests for future Feathly products such as IMS, WMS, and workflow tools

What not to post publicly

  • Google Play purchase email or order ID
  • Refund requests, receipts, or payment details
  • Private screenshots containing personal information
  • Smart Planner backup or database files
  • Security details that could put users or systems at risk

Use feathly.com Smart Planner Support for private requests. The form sends a structured message to support@feathly.com.

Community direction

Feathly aims to build practical AI-assisted software that reduces cost and gives more value back to users and small teams.
