Sync Notice

Smart Planner v1 is local-first. Online sync and backup are not included in the first release.

Your Smart Planner data is stored locally on your device in v1. Please export or back up data before changing devices or uninstalling.

Not included in v1

  • Feathly account
  • Multi-device sync
  • Online backup and restore
  • Server-side loop storage

Possible future features

Future versions may introduce backup, multi-device sync, restore, account migration, or shared server storage.

Future pricing

Online features create ongoing server costs, so they may require a separate fee or subscription with clear notice.
