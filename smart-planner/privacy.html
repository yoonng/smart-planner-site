Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-18. This policy explains how Smart Planner by Feathly handles your data.

Smart Planner v1 is designed as a local-first app. Your loop content is stored on your device unless you choose to export or share it.

1. Local-first data storage

Smart Planner v1 stores your app data locally on your device. This may include loop titles, notes, categories, templates, schedule settings, reminder times, review records, completion history, app preferences, language, theme, wake and sleep time, notification window, time zone settings, and local premium purchase status used to unlock paid features.

In Smart Planner v1, Feathly does not operate a cloud sync server for your loop data, and your loop content is not uploaded to Feathly servers.

2. Account and cloud sync

Smart Planner v1 does not require a Feathly account and does not provide real-time cloud sync. If cloud sync, cloud backup, account login, or server-based features are added in the future, this policy will be updated before those features are used.

3. Purchases and PRO status

If you purchase a premium or lifetime feature, the payment is processed through Google Play. Feathly does not directly collect or store your full payment card details. Smart Planner may store local purchase status, product ID, purchase time, purchase restoration status, and encrypted entitlement information on your device to unlock PRO features and remove ads.

If you choose to link a Google account as a purchase account hint, Smart Planner may store a masked email address and an encrypted local copy of that email on your device. This is used only to help you remember which account may have been used when restoring a purchase. It is not uploaded to Feathly servers in v1.

4. Advertising

The free version of Smart Planner may display a bottom banner ad through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect or process advertising identifiers, device information, app activity, diagnostics, and related data according to Google's advertising and privacy policies. Early Supporter PRO users can remove ads by purchasing PRO.

Smart Planner does not use interstitial ads, app-open ads, rewarded ads, or pop-up ads in v1.

5. Notifications

Smart Planner may request notification permission so the app can send loop reminders. Notification scheduling is used to support the reminder features you set up in the app. You can change notification permissions in your device settings.

6. Export and import

If Smart Planner provides export or import features, exported files are controlled by you. You are responsible for where you save, share, or back up those files.

7. Data sharing

Feathly v1 does not sell your personal data. Smart Planner v1 does not upload your loop content to Feathly servers or share your loop content with advertisers. Google Play may process purchase-related information when you make a purchase through Google Play, and Google AdMob may process advertising-related data when ads are shown in the free version.

8. Data deletion

Because Smart Planner v1 stores data locally, you can remove local app data by deleting loops inside the app, clearing app data, or uninstalling the app. If you uninstall the app without exporting or backing up your data, your local Smart Planner data may be lost.

Smart Planner v1 does not provide a Feathly cloud account, so there is no Feathly server account deletion process in this version. If account or cloud features are added later, account and cloud data deletion options will be provided.

9. Security

Smart Planner is designed as a local-first app. Purchase entitlement and optional purchase account hint information may be stored locally using encrypted storage where supported. Your data security also depends on your device security settings, such as screen lock, device encryption, backups, and operating system protection.

10. Children

Smart Planner is a general productivity and study planning app. It is not specifically designed for children under the age required by local law to use digital services without parental consent. If you are under that age, please use Smart Planner with permission from a parent or guardian.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as Smart Planner changes. Important changes, especially those related to ads, purchases, cloud sync, accounts, or server-based data handling, will be communicated clearly.

12. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@feathly.com.
