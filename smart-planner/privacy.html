Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-16. This policy explains how Smart Planner by Feathly handles your data.

Smart Planner v1 is designed as a local-first app. Your loop content is stored on your device unless you choose to export or share it.

1. Local-first data storage

Smart Planner v1 stores your app data locally on your device. This may include loop titles, notes, categories, templates, schedule settings, reminder times, review records, completion history, app preferences, language, theme, wake and sleep time, notification window, time zone settings, and local premium purchase status used to unlock paid features.

In Smart Planner v1, Feathly does not operate a cloud sync server for your loop data, and your loop content is not uploaded to Feathly servers.

2. Account and cloud sync

Smart Planner v1 does not require a Feathly account and does not provide real-time cloud sync. If cloud sync, cloud backup, account login, or server-based features are added in the future, this policy will be updated before those features are used.

3. Purchases

If you purchase a premium or lifetime feature, the payment is processed through Google Play. Feathly does not directly collect or store your full payment card details. Smart Planner may store purchase status information on your device to unlock premium features and support purchase restoration.

4. Notifications

Smart Planner may request notification permission so the app can send loop reminders. Notification scheduling is used to support the reminder features you set up in the app. You can change notification permissions in your device settings.

5. Export and import

If Smart Planner provides export or import features, exported files are controlled by you. You are responsible for where you save, share, or back up those files.

6. Data sharing

Feathly v1 does not sell your personal data. Smart Planner v1 does not share your loop content with advertisers. Google Play may process purchase-related information according to Google Play policies when you make a purchase through Google Play.

7. Data deletion

Because Smart Planner v1 stores data locally, you can remove local app data by deleting loops inside the app, clearing app data, or uninstalling the app. If you uninstall the app without exporting or backing up your data, your local Smart Planner data may be lost.

Smart Planner v1 does not provide a Feathly cloud account, so there is no Feathly server account deletion process in this version. If account or cloud features are added later, account and cloud data deletion options will be provided.

8. Security

Smart Planner is designed as a local-first app. Your data security also depends on your device security settings, such as screen lock, device encryption, backups, and operating system protection.

9. Children

Smart Planner is a general productivity and study planning app. It is not specifically designed for children under the age required by local law to use digital services without parental consent. If you are under that age, please use Smart Planner with permission from a parent or guardian.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as Smart Planner changes. Important changes, especially those related to cloud sync, accounts, or server-based data handling, will be communicated clearly.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@feathly.com.
