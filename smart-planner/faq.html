FAQ

Basic answers for Smart Planner v1.

What is a Loop?

A Loop is a repeatable task that comes back at the right time so you do not have to hold it in your head.

What is Smart Planner for?

Smart Planner is built for study, exam preparation, routines, repeated work, and calm reminders.

What is Focus Timer?

Focus Timer is a focus and break timer that can be used with your loops. It is intended to support work sessions without making the app complicated.

Where is my data stored?

In v1, Smart Planner stores your loop data locally on your device. Export your data before changing devices, clearing app data, or uninstalling.

Does it support cloud sync?

Not in v1. Cloud sync and backup may be added later, and server-based features may require a separate fee.

What is Early Supporter PRO?

Early Supporter PRO is a one-time purchase for current local premium features. It supports early development.
