1. About Smart Planner

Smart Planner by Feathly is a smart loop planner that helps you create spaced loops, routine loops, reminders, focus sessions, and review records. It is designed to support study, memory, habits, and personal planning.

Smart Planner v1 is a local-first app. Your loop data is stored on your device unless you choose to export, back up, or share it using features provided by your device or the app.

3. Reminder and loop model

Smart Planner uses generated reminder occurrences for Home counts, Home sections, and Insight statistics. In step-based spaced loops, the next reminder may be generated after you complete the current step. Future pattern steps are not guaranteed to appear or be counted before they are actually generated.

The Home screen may group generated reminders into NOW, TODAY, LATER, and WAITING. These groups are provided to help you understand what is due now, what remains later today, what is scheduled after today, and which active loops are waiting without a current unfinished reminder.
