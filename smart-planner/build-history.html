Build History

Release notes and public build status for Smart Planner.

Smart Planner is currently in Android launch preparation. Public release notes will be updated as builds become available.

Current status

Android v1 preparation

Status: internal testing and release preparation.

  • Local-first loop planner foundation
  • Step-based spaced loop model: the next reminder is generated after the current step is completed
  • Home screen sections: NOW, TODAY, LATER, and WAITING
  • Home statistics and Insights now align around generated reminder occurrences
  • Routine Loop and Review Loop support
  • Basic PRO feature and Google Play purchase integration preparation
  • Data safety, support, privacy, and terms pages prepared

Recent internal update

Occurrence-based Home and Insights model

Smart Planner now treats generated reminder rows as the public unit for Home counts and Insight statistics. Potential future pattern steps are not counted until they are actually generated.

  • Done: completed generated reminders in today's operational day
  • NOW: generated reminders that are due now or overdue
  • TODAY: generated reminders later within today's operational day
  • LATER: generated reminders after today
  • WAITING: active loops with no unfinished generated reminder

Planned platform path

  • Android: first release target
  • iOS: in development
  • Tablet, PC, and Mac: planned for sequential release

Notes

Build notes may change during testing. The goal is to keep public release information clear and simple for users.
