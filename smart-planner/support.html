Contact Support

Need help with Smart Planner by Feathly?

Contact

Contact us by email: support@feathly.com

Please include

  • App version
  • Device model
  • Android version
  • A short description of the issue
  • Screenshots if helpful
  • Steps to reproduce the problem, if possible

Purchase support

If your premium purchase is not showing correctly, try Restore Purchase first. Make sure you are using the same Google Play account that made the purchase.

If the issue continues, contact support and include the approximate purchase date.

Official support scope

Official support is intended for apps installed through Google Play or another distribution channel explicitly approved by Feathly, and for data created or modified through the app's official features.

Unsupported cases

Support may be limited or unavailable for issues caused by modified or repackaged apps, unofficial distribution files, direct SQLite database edits, external database tools, unofficial backup files, rooted or otherwise abnormal execution environments, or unofficial changes to app data, purchase state, or internal files.

Local data and backup

Smart Planner v1 stores planner data locally on your device. If you delete the app, clear app data, or restore an unofficial backup, local data may be lost or become inconsistent. Use official export or backup features when available.

Download and build status

See the Download page for platform availability and the Build History page for current release status.

Response time

Smart Planner is an early-stage app. We will try to respond as soon as possible, but response times may vary depending on workload and time zone.
