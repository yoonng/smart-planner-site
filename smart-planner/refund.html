Refund Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-18. This Refund Policy applies to purchases made for Smart Planner by Feathly through Google Play.

1. Google Play purchases

Smart Planner Android purchases are processed through Google Play. Refund eligibility, refund timing, payment reversal, taxes, and payment method handling are generally managed according to Google Play policies and applicable law.

2. Lifetime purchase

If Smart Planner offers a lifetime purchase, it is a one-time digital purchase that unlocks the premium features described in the app at the time of purchase.

A lifetime purchase covers the applicable local premium features of Smart Planner, such as ad removal and other current local PRO benefits. Future cloud sync, cloud backup, server storage, multi-device sync, or other server-based features may require a separate fee or subscription if ongoing server costs are involved.

3. How to request a refund

If you want to request a refund for a Google Play purchase, please use the refund process provided by Google Play. You may also contact Feathly support if you need help understanding the purchase or restoring access.

Support email: support@feathly.com

4. Restore purchase

If you bought Smart Planner PRO but the app does not show PRO access, please try the Restore Purchase option in the app first. Make sure you are using the same Google Play account that made the purchase.

If you use multiple Google accounts, check the Google Play account used for the original purchase before requesting a refund or contacting support.

5. Purchase account hint

If you optionally saved a purchase account hint inside the app, it may help you remember which Google account may have been used. This hint is not a Feathly server account and does not replace Google Play's purchase records.

6. Support limitations

Feathly can help investigate purchase access issues and guide you to the correct Google Play refund path. However, because payment is processed through Google Play, some refund decisions and payment processing steps may be controlled by Google Play and your payment provider.

7. Unauthorized purchases

If you believe a purchase was unauthorized, please check your Google Play account and payment method activity and follow Google Play's process for unauthorized charges. You may also contact Feathly support for guidance.
