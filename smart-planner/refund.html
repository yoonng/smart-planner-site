Refund Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-16. This page explains refund handling for Smart Planner purchases.

Google Play purchases

Smart Planner Android purchases are expected to be processed through Google Play. Refund requests should be submitted through Google Play according to Google Play policies and procedures.

Lifetime purchase

The Early Supporter PRO lifetime purchase is a one-time digital purchase for the current local premium features of Smart Planner. Future cloud sync, cloud backup, server storage, or other server-based features may require a separate fee or subscription.

Before requesting a refund

If premium access does not appear after purchase, please try Restore Purchase using the same Google Play account used for the purchase.

Support

If you need help with purchase access, contact support@feathly.com and include your app version, device model, Android version, and approximate purchase date.
